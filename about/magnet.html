What does it mean to LEAD?

Students are taught to LEAD at Green. This helps students realize the leader in themselves and help others do the same. We are growing leaders for today and for the future!

7 Habits

Students learn to be leaders based on Dr. Covey's 7 Habits. You can hear students using these 7 habits throughout their school day as well as in the community and home.

  1. Be proactive!
    You're in charge
  2. Begin with the end in mind!
    Have a plan
  3. Put first things first!
    Work first, then play
    Then play well with others!
  4. Think win-win
    Everyone can win!
  5. Seek first to understand, then to be understood
    Listen before you talk!
  6. Synergize
    Together is better!
  7. Sharpen the saw
    Balance feels best!
