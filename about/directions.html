Driving Directions

From I-40W:

  1. Head northeast on I-440 E.
  2. Take exit 11 to merge onto US-1 N/US-401 N/Capital Blvd/North Blvd toward Wake Forest/Louisburg
  3. Slight left onto US-1 N
  4. Turn right onto Spring Forest Road
  5. Go through traffic light at Hollenden Drive
  6. Take the immediately left into school parking lot

From the east (Knightdale, Wendell, Zebulon) on I-40E:

  1. Take I-540 West toward Durham
  2. Take exit 18 for US-401 S
  3. Turn left onto US-401 S/Louisburg Rd 0.6 miles
  4. Take the 2nd right onto Spring Forest Rd Destination will be on the right, just before the Hollenden Road light. (Past fire station and daycare)

From the south (Knightdale, Wendell, Zebulon) on I-40E:

  1. Take US-401 North
  2. Slight right to merge onto I-40 E/US-64 E
  3. Continue onto I-440 W
  4. Take exit 11B to merge onto US-1 N/US-401 N/Capital Blvd/North Blvd toward Wake Forest/Louisburg
  5. Slight left onto US-1 N
  6. Take the 1st right onto Spring Forest Rd Destination will be on the right immediately past the Hollenden Road traffic light
